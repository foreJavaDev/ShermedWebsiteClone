Understanding Our

Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

SHERmed is an Occupational Health service provider committed to protecting the privacy of your personal and medical information in accordance with South African privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, and company info
  • Appointment and medical booking details
  • ID/Passport and/or patient records
  • Website usage data (cookies, analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide health screening and compliance services
  • To schedule and manage bookings
  • For communication, invoicing, and support
  • To improve platform performance and security

4. Data Storage & Security

We store your data securely on encrypted servers and only share it with authorized personnel. Access is strictly controlled.

5. Third-Party Access

We do not sell or rent your data. We may use third-party services (e.g., payment processors) who also comply with privacy standards.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic.

8. Contact

For questions or data requests, email info@shermed.co.za or marinda@shermed.co.za
